# Community-Led Growth (CLG) Plan

## 1. Core Principle
Value the **Contributor** as much as the **Attendee**.

## 2. Feature Hooks

### A. Shareable "Interest" Cards
- Developers can share a "Who's in for Pizza?" card on X/LinkedIn with one click.
- The card is dynamic, showing current interested count.

### B. "Super Mucca" Contributor Status
- Automated recognition for developers who contribute to the project's Pest coverage or documentation.
- Badge: `Super Mucca Contributor` 🐄✨

### C. Open Roadmap
- A public voting system for the next features (e.g., "Google Calendar Integration" vs "Photo Gallery").

## 3. Integration with Existing Modules
- **Meetup**: Add `interest_count` and `share_token` to `Event`.
- **User**: Add `points` and `badges` fields to `Profile`.
- **Notify**: Multi-channel alerts for "New meetup interest in your city!".

## 4. Launch "Side Quest"
Launch a campaign: **"The Great Pest Coverage Race"**.
Challenge the community to reach 100% coverage in 30 days. Feature contributors prominently on the homepage.
