# Color Palette Guidelines - Meetup Theme

## Core Principles

- **Contrast is Key**: Ensure sufficient color contrast between text and background to meet WCAG AA standards and improve readability for all users.
- **Brand Consistency**: Adhere to the project's brand colors to maintain a consistent look and feel across the application.
- **Limited Palette**: Use a limited color palette to avoid visual clutter and create a more professional aesthetic.

## Implementation Guidelines

- **Primary Color**: Used for primary actions, links, and highlights.
- **Secondary Color**: Used for secondary actions and less important elements.
- **Accent Color**: Used for highlights and to draw attention to specific elements.
- **Neutral Colors**: A range of grays for text, backgrounds, and borders.
- **Feedback Colors**: Specific colors for success, error, warning, and info messages.

## Example Palette (based on Tailwind CSS)

- **Primary**: `blue-600`
- **Secondary**: `gray-500`
- **Accent**: `pink-500`
- **Text**: `gray-900` (headings), `gray-700` (body)
- **Background**: `white`, `gray-50`
- **Success**: `green-500`
- **Error**: `red-500`
- **Warning**: `yellow-500`
- **Info**: `blue-500`
