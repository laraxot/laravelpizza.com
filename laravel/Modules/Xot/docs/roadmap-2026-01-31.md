## 🎯 Module Purpose
Xot is the foundational framework module of Laraxot PTVX, providing base classes, traits, patterns, and core infrastructure for all other modules in the ecosystem. It serves as the architectural heart, ensuring consistency, extensibility, and maintainability across the entire application.
