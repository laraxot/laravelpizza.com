## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Developer Experience
Make extension development easy and enjoyable.

### Pillar 2: Platform Stability
Extensions never break core functionality.

### Pillar 3: Ecosystem Growth
Build a thriving extension community.

### Pillar 4: Quality Assurance
Maintain high extension standards.
