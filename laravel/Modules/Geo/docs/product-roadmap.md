# Geo Module - Product Roadmap

**Module:** Geo  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Team  
**Status:** In Development

---

## Vision Statement

To provide **comprehensive geographic capabilities** that enable location-based features, regulatory compliance, and localized experiences while respecting user privacy.

### Vision Pillars

1. **Accurate Location:** Precise geographic data and services
2. **Compliance:** Geographic restrictions and regulations
3. **Localization:** Region-specific experiences
4. **Privacy:** Respect user location privacy
5. **Performance:** Fast geographic lookups
