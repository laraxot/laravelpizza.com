### Tool Stack

**CRM**:
- Lead tracking
- Interaction history
- Pipeline management

**Analytics**:
- Google Analytics 4
- Conversion tracking
- Funnel analysis

### Automation Workflows

**Workflow 1: New Lead Magnet Download**
```
Trigger: Download submitted
↓
Email 1: Confirmation + Link (Immediate)
↓
Email 2: Value-add content (Day 2)
↓
Email 3: Social proof (Day 5)
↓
Email 4: Offer (Day 10)
↓
Email 5: Follow-up (Day 15)
↓
Lead scoring update
↓
If not converted → Lead recycling (Day 30)
```

**Workflow 2: Newsletter Subscriber**
```
Trigger: Email confirmed
↓
Welcome sequence (5 emails over 14 days)
↓
Ongoing: Bi-weekly newsletter
↓
Behavioral triggers:
- Clicked link about services → Service-focused sequence
- Downloaded guide → Expert positioning sequence
- Inactive 90 days → Re-engagement campaign
```
