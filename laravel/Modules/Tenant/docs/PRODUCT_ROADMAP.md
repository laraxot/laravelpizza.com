# Tenant Module - Product Roadmap

**Module:** Tenant  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Team  
**Status:** In Development

---

## Vision Statement

To provide a **robust multi-tenancy infrastructure** that enables secure, isolated, and scalable deployment of the platform for multiple organizations while maintaining operational efficiency and data privacy.

### Vision Pillars

1. **Isolation:** Complete data and configuration separation
2. **Scalability:** Support thousands of tenants
3. **Flexibility:** Configurable per-tenant features
4. **Security:** Tenant data protection
5. **Management:** Easy tenant administration
